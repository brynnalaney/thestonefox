The Stone Fox

San Luis Obispo's premier disco club

This club provides an inclusive and fun environment where dance fanatics can let loose, spend time with their loved ones, and purchase beverages. We offer signature 70s inspired cocktails, along with a full service bar. We are a hip and youthful club, inspired by the 70s, that is founded on music and community. Our friendly and passionate staff welcomes visitors, students and locals from all across San Luis Obispo county each night, and always puts the customer first.
